Question: What is your company doing to diversify the field of computer science?

Predicted Response: I predict that their company is diversifying the field of computer science by recruiting minorities in coding to work for them and providing mentorship

Software Developer Response: Great question! Our company sponsors outreach activities to help fund coding classes for people of all ages, has inclusive hiring and recruiting practices, and works to maintain a culture of acceptance.
